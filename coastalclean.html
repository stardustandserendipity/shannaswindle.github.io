Padre Island Counseling is a mental health provider established by Corpus Christi native Johanna Torres. Johanna came to HRMG for a website that would establish her web presence and allow her to showcase her therapeutic services to potential clients. Furthermore, her new business needed help with branding to set her practice apart.
Once the major design decisions were made, the website build itself was fairly straightforward. Web development focused on presenting contact forms to schedule consultations, and communicating necessary information regarding services, insurance, and FAQ’s. Keyword research was conducted in order to appropriately convey information on specialized therapies offered by the practice.
